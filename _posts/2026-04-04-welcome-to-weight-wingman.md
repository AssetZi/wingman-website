Making weight — whether you're cutting for competition or bulking for gains — shouldn't feel like guesswork. That's why we built **Weight Wingman**.

## What Is Weight Wingman?

Weight Wingman is a fitness companion app designed to take the complexity out of weight management. Whether you're an athlete preparing for a weigh-in, someone looking to lose a few pounds, or a lifter trying to add muscle, the app builds a plan around *your* goals.

## What You Get

### Personalized Meal Plans
No generic diet templates. Weight Wingman creates meal plans tailored to your specific targets — calories, macros, and food preferences all factored in.

### Smart Shopping Lists
Every meal plan comes with a ready-made shopping list. Walk into the grocery store knowing exactly what you need. No more wandering the aisles or impulse buying.

### Progress Tracking
Log your weight and track your journey over time. See trends, stay accountable, and celebrate the wins along the way.

### AI-Powered Photo Analysis
Snap a photo of your plate and let the app analyze your meal. It's a quick way to stay honest about what you're eating without manually logging every ingredient.

### Built with Apple Intelligence
Weight Wingman leverages Apple's Foundation Models to deliver smart, on-device insights. Your data never leaves your phone — privacy is baked in.

## Getting Started

1. [Download Weight Wingman](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/weight-wingman/id6740700573) from the App Store
2. Set your goal — cut, bulk, or maintain
3. Get your personalized meal plan
4. Hit the grocery store with your shopping list
5. Track your progress and let AI keep you on course
